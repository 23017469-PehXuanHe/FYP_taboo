How to Play

  1. Start the game by entering team names.
  2. One player describes the main (Taboo) word without using any forbidden words listed.
  3. If the guesser says the correct word, click ✔ Got It! and the team scores.
  4. If the guesser says a forbidden word or runs out of time (30s), the other team scores.
  5. Use the input box to guess the word or play by voice/chat if in person.
  6. Use the Add Custom Card feature on the homepage to create your own words!
